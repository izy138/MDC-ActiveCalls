Map Information

This map is a visualization of live Miami-Dade Fire Rescue and FHP traffic calls. It is for informational use only and not an official dispatch system.

Location accuracy

  • MDFR addresses are often reported as street blocks or intersections, not exact premises. Geocoded points are approximate and may fall near the center of the reported block.
  • When an MDFR address cannot be geocoded, the marker is placed near the center of the reported response zone.
  • FHP incidents use latitude/longitude from the FHP feed but may still be approximate.

Usage

  • Do not use this map for emergency response, navigation, or life-safety decisions. Always dial 911 for emergencies.
  • Call counts and locations update automatically every 60 seconds; short delays or discrepancies with official systems are expected.
